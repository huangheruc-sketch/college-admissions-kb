## 申请材料

### 一、申请系统：UC Application

| 项目 | 要求 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 申请平台 | **UC Application** | Berkeley 属于 UC 系统，**不是 Common App** |
| 申请对象 | First-year applicants | 通过 UC 统一系统提交，再由 Berkeley 审核 |
| 成绩提交方式 | Self-reported | 申请阶段自报课程与成绩；录取入学后核验正式成绩单 |
| 推荐信 | 非常规必需材料 | Berkeley CDS 写明 recommendations 为 Considered，但常规申请流程并非人人必须提交；若学校另行 request，再按指示补交 |
| 作品集 / résumé | 常规审查不看 | Berkeley Selection Process 页写明 art portfolios、résumés 等通常不纳入常规申请审查 |

### 二、学术准备要求

| 项目 | 要求 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 高中毕业 | 必需 | CDS C3：High school diploma is required and GED is accepted |
| 大学预备课程 | 必需 | CDS C4：Require |
| 总学术单元 | 15 required / 18 recommended | CDS C5 |
| 英语 | 4 年 | Required |
| 数学 | 3 年 required / 4 年 recommended | 工程方向官方鼓励修到 calculus |
| 实验科学 | 2 年 required / 3 年 recommended | Engineering 官方特别强调 math & science strength |
| 外语 | 2 年 required / 3 年 recommended | CDS C5 |
| 历史 | 2 年 | CDS C5 |
| 视觉/表演艺术 | 1 年 | CDS C5 |

## 文书

### UC PIQ（Personal Insight Questions）

**要求：** 8 选 4，每篇 **最多 350 词**。

| 题号 | Prompt |
|---|---|
| 1 | Describe an example of your leadership experience in which you have positively influenced others, helped resolve disputes or contributed to group efforts over time. |
| 2 | Every person has a creative side, and it can be expressed in many ways: problem solving, original and innovative thinking, and artistically, to name a few. Describe how you express your creative side. |
| 3 | What would you say is your greatest talent or skill? How have you developed and demonstrated that talent over time? |
| 4 | Describe how you have taken advantage of a significant educational opportunity or worked to overcome an educational barrier you have faced. |
| 5 | Describe the most significant challenge you have faced and the steps you have taken to overcome this challenge. How has this challenge affected your academic achievement? |
| 6 | Think about an academic subject that inspires you. Describe how you have furthered this interest inside and/or outside of the classroom. |
| 7 | What have you done to make your school or your community a better place? |
| 8 | Beyond what has already been shared in your application, what do you believe makes you a strong candidate for admissions to the University of California? |

**官方说明要点：**
- 8 题中只答 4 题；所有题目权重相同。
- 每篇上限 350 词。
- 可在 additional comments 栏补充异常情况，但该栏不应替代正式文书。

## 录取标准

### Berkeley / UC 审核方式

| 项目 | 政策 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 录取方式 | **Holistic review** | Berkeley Selection Process 明确：first-year 采用 holistic review |
| 是否按单一指标录取 | 否 | 官方写明 no one piece of information is weighted more heavily over another |
| Early round | 无 | Berkeley Dates & Deadlines 明确不提供 early admission / early decision |
| 专业录取 | 视学院/项目而定 | Berkeley Engineering 明确要求申请 specific program；EECS 为 direct-admit major |

### CDS C7 录取因素（最新口径）

| 因素 | Berkeley CDS 2025-26 |
|---|---|
| 课程难度（Rigor） | Very Important |
| GPA | Very Important |
| 文书（Application Essay / PIQ） | Very Important |
| 标化成绩 | Not considered for admission, even if submitted |
| 推荐信 | Considered |
| 面试 | Not Considered |
| 课外活动 | Important |
| 品格 / 个人特质 | Important |
| 志愿活动 | Important |
| 工作经验 | Important |
| First-generation | Considered |
| 州居民身份 | Considered |
| 校友关系 | Not Considered |
| 地理来源 | Not Considered |
| Demonstrated Interest | Not Considered |

## 标化考试

| 项目 | 政策 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| SAT / ACT | **Test-free** | Berkeley/UC 当前不把 SAT/ACT 用于录取决定；2025-26 CDS 亦写明 Not considered for admission, even if submitted |
| AP / IB | 可作为学术背景组成部分 | 不是 SAT/ACT 替代关系；按 UC 课程与成绩体系整体看 |
| 英语考试（国际生） | 仅用于英语能力要求 | 与 SAT/ACT 录取政策不同 |

> 注：CDS 表头中仍保留“Does your institution make use of SAT or ACT scores”历史字段，但 Berkeley 在具体政策栏明确填写 **Not considered for admission, even if submitted**；对申请人应按 **test-free** 理解。

## 面试

| 项目 | 说明 |
|---|---|
| 是否提供常规招生面试 | **否** |
| 官方表述 | Berkeley 官网写明：UC Berkeley does not offer alumni or admissions officer interviews as part of its application process |
| 例外说明 | 国际生可自愿提交 InitialView 材料，但为 optional，且**不是 Berkeley 面试要求** |
