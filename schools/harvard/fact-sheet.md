## 申请材料

### 一、Common App 通用部分

> 以下为 Common Application 所有学校通用的填写模块。Harvard 同时接受 Coalition Application, Powered by Scoir。

#### Profile（个人信息）
- 法定姓名、常用姓名、出生日期、性别、国籍/公民身份、出生国家、家庭住址、联系方式（邮箱/电话）
- Demographics 部分非必填

#### Family（家庭信息）
- 父母/监护人姓名、职业、教育水平、联系方式
- 兄弟姐妹姓名与年龄
- 即使为成人或 emancipated minor 也需填写

#### Education（教育信息）
- 当前高中名称、学校类型、入学/毕业时间
- 是否就读过其他高中
- 大学课程经历（如有）：最多 3 所，类型包括 Dual Enrollment / Summer Program / Credit Awarded Directly by College
- GPA、班级规模、班级排名
- **当前学年课程**（Current Year Courses）：列出课程名称 + Level
  - Level 选项：Regular, Accelerated, Advanced, AP, A-Level, College Prep, Dual Enrollment, Enriched, GCSE, Gifted, High Honors, Intensive, IB, Pre-IB, Regents
- **学术荣誉**（Honors）：最多 **5 项**，每项填写 Title、Grade Level、Level of Recognition，无详细说明空间
- Community-Based Organizations：填写提供过免费申请帮助的社区组织
- **Future Plans**：计划申请年份、最高学历目标、感兴趣的职业方向（单选）

#### Testing（考试）
- 可自报成绩（self-reported scores），提交后可通过 Applicant Portal 更新
- 不应填写 practice test scores
- 可选考试项目：ACT, SAT/SAT Subject, AP, IB, Cambridge, TOEFL, PTE Academic, IELTS, Duolingo English Test

#### Activities（活动）
- 最多 **10 项**活动
- 每项填写：类别（从下方列表选）、职位描述（**50 词**）、活动及成就描述（**150 词**）、参与年级、每周时间、每年周数
- 类别列表：Academic, Art, Athletics: Club, Career Oriented, Community Service (Volunteer), Computer/Technology, Cultural, Dance, Debate/Speech, Environmental, Family Responsibilities, Foreign Exchange, Foreign Language, Internship, Journalism/Publication, Junior R.O.T.C., LGBT, Music: Instrumental, Music: Vocal, Religious, Research, Robotics, School Spirit, Science/Math, Social Justice, Student Govt./Politics, Theater/Drama, Work (Paid), Other Club/Activity
- **Responsibilities and Circumstances**：说明活动参与是否受家庭或其他情况影响

#### Writing（写作）
- 包括 Personal Essay 和 Additional Information 两部分
- Additional Information：说明可能影响学业表现的特殊情况（如住房不稳定、家庭变故、自然灾害、健康问题等）

#### Recommenders and FERPA
- 签署 FERPA 授权（通常选择放弃查看推荐信权利）
- 输入推荐人和 counselor 邮箱，由系统发出邀请

### 二、Harvard 专属部分

#### Harvard Supplement 字段

| 项目 | 要求 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 意向专业方向（Intended Concentration） | 最多 3 个，按兴趣排序 | Harvard 本科不按专业录取 |
| 明确度自评 | 必填 | 分别评估学术计划、职业计划、课外/体育兴趣的明确程度 |
| 在 Harvard 想参加的活动 | 必填 | 按偏好排序；体育需注明级别 |
| 体育参与级别 | 视所填活动 | Intercollegiate 或 Club/Intramural/Recreational |
| 补充材料（Supplementary Materials） | 可选 | 可提交 video / audio / images，完全 optional |
| NEC / Berklee 联合项目 | 视情况 | Yes/No；官方建议不要同时申请两个 |
| 如何了解 Harvard | 必填 | 按影响排序填 3 项；仅用于评估 recruitment，不作为 demonstrated interest |

**「如何了解 Harvard」选项列表：**
Alumni, Campus Visit, College Counselor/Teacher, College Fair, Current Harvard Student, Email, Family Member, Harvard Admissions Visit to Your Hometown, Harvard Website, Letter/Mailing, Other Website, Social Media, Virtual Event

#### 学校材料（由学校提交）

| 材料 | 提交人 | 时间 |
|---|---|---|
| Secondary School Report + 成绩单 | Counselor / school leader | 随申请提交 |
| Midyear School Report | Counselor / school official | 高三上学期结束后 |
| Final School Report | Counselor | 录取并入学者须在 7/1 前提交 |

## 文书

### Common App 主文书（Personal Essay）

**要求：** 250–650 词，7 个 prompt 选 1

| 题号 | Prompt |
|---|---|
| 1 | Some students have a background, identity, interest, or talent that is so meaningful they believe their application would be incomplete without it. If this sounds like you, then please share your story. |
| 2 | The lessons we take from obstacles we encounter can be fundamental to later success. Recount a time when you faced a challenge, setback, or failure. How did it affect you, and what did you learn from the experience? |
| 3 | Reflect on a time when you questioned or challenged a belief or idea. What prompted your thinking? What was the outcome? |
| 4 | Reflect on something that someone has done for you that has made you happy or thankful in a surprising way. How has this gratitude affected or motivated you? |
| 5 | Discuss an accomplishment, event, or realization that sparked a period of personal growth and a new understanding of yourself or others. |
| 6 | Describe a topic, idea, or concept you find so engaging that it makes you lose all track of time. Why does it captivate you? What or who do you turn to when you want to learn more? |
| 7 | Share an essay on any topic of your choice. It can be one you've already written, one that responds to a different prompt, or one of your own design. |

### Harvard Supplement 短答题（Short Questions）

**要求：** 每题 **150 词**，共 5 题，全部必答

| 题号 | Prompt |
|---|---|
| 1 | Harvard has long recognized the importance of enrolling a diverse student body. How will the life experiences that shape who you are today enable you to contribute to Harvard? |
| 2 | Describe a time when you strongly disagreed with someone about an idea or issue. How did you communicate or engage with this person? What did you learn from this experience? |
| 3 | Briefly describe any of your extracurricular activities, employment experience, travel, or family responsibilities that have shaped who you are. |
| 4 | How do you hope to use your Harvard education in the future? |
| 5 | Top 3 things your roommates might like to know about you. |

> ⚠️ 第 1 题为 Harvard 官方 supplement PDF 当前版本，与部分第三方网站流传版本不同。以 Harvard 官网 PDF 为准。

## 推荐信

| 类型 | 是否必需 | 要求 |
|---|---|---|
| 教师推荐信（Teacher Evaluation） | ✅ 必需，2 封 | 须来自**不同学科**的核心学术课老师 |
| 学校报告含 Counselor 推荐 | ✅ 必需 | 随 Secondary School Report 一并提交 |
| 额外推荐信（Additional Recommendation） | 可选 | 可在提交申请后追加；来自了解你的其他推荐人 |

**操作流程：**
1. 尽早确认推荐老师，当面或邮件沟通
2. 在 Common App「Recommenders and FERPA」模块签署 FERPA、输入推荐人邮箱
3. 学校材料通常可在截止日后约一周内到达

## 标化考试

| 项目 | 政策 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| SAT / ACT | **必须提交（Required）** | 可自报成绩；录取后入学前须提交官方成绩单验证 |
| 送分代码 | SAT: **3434**；ACT: **1840** | — |
| 考试不可达情形 | 可用替代考试 | 如 AP、IB、GCSE/A-Level、National Leaving Exams 替代 |
| AP / IB | 不作为 SAT/ACT 替代（正常情况下） | 但可作为学术实力补充参考 |
| 英语考试（TOEFL 等） | 不能替代 SAT/ACT | 本页服务对象为美国公民，通常不需要 |
| 成绩更新 | 提交后可通过 Applicant Portal 更新 | 不建议提交 practice test scores |

## 面试

| 项目 | 说明 |
|---|---|
| 是否提供面试 | 是，但**不是所有申请者都会被分配** |
| 是否必需 | 否。官方明确表示未获面试**不影响完整审查** |
| 能否主动申请 | **不能**。由招生委员会根据资源与裁量分配 |
| 安排方式 | 由当地校友面试官通过 email 联系申请者 |
| 面试时间 | 申请提交后；具体时间取决于分配 |
| 官方表述 | "Not receiving an interview will in no way disadvantage a candidate. An application is considered complete without an interview." |

## 提交后流程

1. **Portal 确认邮件**：提交后数天内（9 月中旬起陆续发送）收到 Harvard applicant portal 登录信息
2. **材料追踪**：在 portal 中检查成绩单、推荐信、考试成绩是否收齐
3. **补件**：如显示缺件，联系学校或 College Board / ACT 补交
4. **校友面试**：如被分配，会收到 email 或电话联系
5. **等待结果**：REA 12 月中旬 / RD 3 月底
