## 申请材料

### 一、申请系统选择

Columbia 接受以下三种申请系统，无偏好：
1. **Common Application**
2. **Coalition Application, Powered by Scoir**
3. **QuestBridge Application**（仅限 QuestBridge Finalists）

### 二、Common App / Coalition 通用部分

与 Harvard 相同的 Common App 通用模块（Profile、Family、Education、Testing、Activities、Writing、Recommenders and FERPA）。详见 Harvard Fact Sheet 中的 Common App 通用部分描述。

### 三、Columbia 专属部分

#### 申请选择

申请时须选择 **Columbia College**（文理学院）或 **Columbia Engineering**（SEAS）之一。两者共享校园、课程、社团和大学服务，但课程要求不同。

#### 学校材料

| 材料 | 提交人 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| Secondary School Report + 成绩单 | Counselor | 含所有就读过的高中的官方成绩单 |
| Counselor 推荐信 + School Profile | Counselor | — |
| Midyear Report | Counselor | 高三上学期成绩出来后提交 |
| Final Report + 最终成绩单 | Counselor | 录取并入学者 6 月底前提交 |

## 文书

### Common App / Coalition 主文书（Personal Essay）

**要求：** 250–650 词，7 个 prompt 选 1（与所有 Common App 学校共用，prompts 同 Harvard Fact Sheet）

### Columbia-Specific Questions（Writing Supplement）

**2025–2026 申请周期（Class of 2030）共 6 题，全部必答：**

| 题号 | Prompt | 字数 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | List a selection of texts, resources and outlets that have contributed to your intellectual development outside of academic courses, including but not limited to books, journals, websites, podcasts, essays, plays, presentations, videos, museums and other content that you enjoy. | 100 词（列表格式，逗号/分号分隔，无需作者名或解释） |
| 2 | Tell us about an aspect of your life so far or your lived experience that is important to you, and describe how it has shaped the way you would learn from and contribute to Columbia's multidimensional and collaborative environment. | 150 词 |
| 3 | At Columbia, students representing a wide range of perspectives are invited to live and learn together. In such a community, questions and debates naturally arise. Please describe a time when you did not agree with someone and discuss how you engaged with them and what you took away from the interaction. | 150 词 |
| 4 | In college/university, students are often challenged in ways that they could not anticipate. Please describe a situation in which you have navigated through adversity and discuss how you changed as a result. | 150 词 |
| 5 | Why are you interested in attending Columbia University? We encourage you to consider the aspect(s) that you find unique and compelling about Columbia. | 150 词 |
| 6 | What attracts you to your preferred areas of study at Columbia College or Columbia Engineering? | 150 词 |

> ⚠️ 第 1 题为列表题，官方明确指引：逗号或分号分隔，无需编号、无需斜体、无需作者名或副标题。

## 提交后流程

1. **Applicant Portal**：提交后登录 Columbia 申请者门户检查材料状态
2. **材料追踪**：检查成绩单、推荐信是否收齐
3. **补件**：学校材料可在截止日后到达，无需 counselor 额外申请延期
4. **Midyear Report**：高三上学期成绩出来后提交
5. **等待结果**：ED 12 月中旬 / RD 3 月底
6. **所有录取决定通过 Applicant Portal 发布**，不通过邮件通知
