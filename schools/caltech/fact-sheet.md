# Caltech Fact Sheet

## 时间表

| 项目 | 时间 / 节点 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 申请系统开放（Application Opens） | 夏季后开放 | 可通过 **Common App** 或 **QuestBridge** 申请 |
| Restrictive Early Action 截止 | 11 月 1 日 | 非绑定（non-binding） |
| REA 补件截止 | 11 月 6 日 | required / supplemental materials 可延至该日 |
| REA 标化考试截止 | 11 月 30 日前完成考试 | 官网单列说明 |
| Regular Decision 截止 | 1 月 5 日 | — |
| RD 补件截止 | 1 月 11 日 | required / supplemental materials 可延至该日 |
| RD 标化考试截止 | 12 月 31 日前完成考试 | — |
| REA 放榜 | 12 月中旬 | admit / defer / deny |
| RD 放榜 | 3 月中旬 | admit / waitlist / deny |
| 最终确认入学截止（Reply Deadline） | 5 月 1 日 | 录取后统一截止 |

---

## 申请材料

### 一、申请系统
- Common App 或 QuestBridge Application
- $75 申请费或 fee waiver
- Caltech Specific Questions（补充文书）

### 二、学校与学术材料

| 材料 | 提交人 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| School Report | Counselor | 提供学校背景信息 |
| Secondary School Transcript | School official | 9–11 年级（或国际课程等价） |
| College Transcript（如有） | 学校 / 学生上传 | 高中阶段修读大学课程者应补充 |
| Mid-Year Report | Counselor / school | 高三第一学期成绩可用后提交 |
| AP / IB 成绩 | 学生自报 | 若已参加，会要求报告 |

### 三、推荐信

| 类型 | 是否必需 | 要求 |
|---|---|---|
| STEM 推荐信 | ✅ 必需，1 封 | 来自 Calculus（或更高）、Chemistry、Physics 或 Biology |
| Humanities / Social Sciences 推荐信 | ✅ 必需，1 封 | 来自 English、History、Government 或 Economics |
| Counselor Letter | 可附 | counselor 可随 School Report 额外附推荐 |

### 四、补充材料（可选）
- Research paper / publication
- STEM Portfolio
- Non-STEM Portfolio
- 截止后通过 **Beaver Breakroom** portal 上传

## 文书

### Common App 主文书
- Caltech 允许使用 Common App 主文书；主文书本身仍为 Common App 通用部分

### Caltech Supplemental Essays（Fall 2026）

#### Required STEM Academic Interest Question

| Prompt | 字数 |
|---|---|
| If you had to choose an area of interest or two today, what would you choose? Note that you are under no obligation to follow this choice if admitted. Why did you choose your proposed area of interest? If you selected 'other', what topics are you interested in pursuing? | Min 100 / Max 200 |

#### Required Short Essay Questions

| 题号 | Prompt | 字数 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Regardless of your STEM interest listed above, take this opportunity to nerd out and talk to us about whatever STEM rabbit hole you have found yourself falling into. Be as specific or broad as you would like. | Min 50 / Max 150 |
| 2A | Tell us how you initially found your interest and passion for science or for a particular STEM topic, and how you have pursued or developed your interest or passion over the last few years. | Min 100 / Max 200 |
| 2B | Tell us about a meaningful STEM-related experience from the last few years and share how and why it inspired your curiosity. | Min 100 / Max 200 |
| 3 | The creativity, inventiveness, and innovation of Caltech's students, faculty, and researchers have won Nobel Prizes and put rovers on Mars. But Techers also innovate in smaller-scale ways everyday... How have you been a creator, inventor, or innovator in your own life? | Min 100 / Max 200 |

> 其中 2A / 2B 二选一。

#### Required Short Answer Questions
- 以下四题中选 **2 题** 作答
- 两个回答合计建议不超过 **250 词**；官网提供两个文本框，各自上限 249 词

| Prompt |
|---|
| What is an interest or hobby you do for fun, and why does it bring you joy? |
| If you could teach a class on any topic or concept, what would it be and why? |
| What is a core piece of your identity or being that shapes how you view and/or interact with the world? |
| What is a concept that blew your mind or baffled you when you first encountered it? |

#### Optional Academic Short Answer Question

| Prompt |
|---|
| OPTIONAL: Have you had any extenuating circumstances (such as limited course selection or disruptions), that have affected your coursework, but that are not described elsewhere in your application? If so, tell us about them here. |

## 标化考试

| 项目 | 政策 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| SAT / ACT | **必须提交（Required）** | Caltech 要求 first-year applicant 提交 SAT 或 ACT |
| ACT Writing / Science | 不要求 | Fall 2026 不要求 ACT writing 或 science subscores |
| 评估方式 | Bucket system | 750–800 / 33–36 会按 bucket 呈现给招生委员会 |
| AP / IB | 如已参加则报告 | 用于补充学术准备度 |
| 英语考试 | 国际申请人要求 / 强烈相关 | DET / IELTS / TOEFL |

## 面试

| 项目 | 说明 |
|---|---|
| 是否要求面试 | **—** |
| 官网一线 first-year 要求页 | 未将 alumni interview 列为标准必需组件 |
| 实操建议 | 以当年 portal / applicant communications 为准 |

> 注：本次官网采集到的 first-year deadlines、requirements、essays、testing 页面中，未见 Caltech 将面试列为常规必需或常规保证提供项目，因此此处标「—」，避免写入未经官网明示的流程。

## 提交后流程

1. **Portal 开通**：截止后会收到 Caltech application portal **Beaver Breakroom** 链接
2. **补充材料上传**：研究、作品集等 optional materials 在 Beaver Breakroom 中按说明提交
3. **Mid-Year Report**：高三第一学期成绩可用后补交
4. **结果发布**：REA 为 12 月中旬；RD 为 3 月中旬
5. **Waitlist**：RD waitlist 学生通常需在 4 月中旬前 opt-in；若 5 月 1 日后有空位，Caltech 会继续审核至 7 月中旬前后结束
