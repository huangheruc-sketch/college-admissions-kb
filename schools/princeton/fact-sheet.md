## 申请材料

### 一、Common App 通用部分

> Princeton 使用 **Common Application**，并要求额外提交 **Princeton-specific Questions**。

### 二、Princeton 专属部分

| 材料 | 是否必需 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| Princeton-specific Questions | ✅ 必需 | 通过 Common App 提交 |
| Graded Written Paper | ✅ 必需 | 最好是英语/历史/社科类说明文写作 |
| Application Fee / Fee Waiver | ✅ 必需 | 申请费或减免 |

### 三、学校与推荐材料

| 材料 | 提交人 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| Transcript | School counselor / official | 官方成绩单 |
| School Report (SR) | Counselor / school official | 与 Counselor Recommendation 分开 |
| Counselor Recommendation | Counselor / adviser | 官方 checklist 单列 |
| Two Teacher Recommendations | 两位老师 | 应来自不同核心学科领域的高阶课程老师 |
| Midyear School Report | Counselor / school official | 高三上成绩可用后提交 |
| Final grades / Final report | School | 录取后要求提交 |

## 文书

### Common App 主文书
- Princeton 沿用 Common App 主文书（250–650 词，7 选 1）

### Princeton-specific Questions

#### 学术兴趣题（按学位路径二选一）

| 类别 | Prompt | 字数 |
|---|---|---|
| A.B. / Undecided | As a research institution that also prides itself on its liberal arts curriculum, Princeton allows students to explore areas across the humanities and the arts, the natural sciences, and the social sciences. What academic areas most pique your curiosity, and how do the programs offered at Princeton suit your particular interests? | 250 词以内 |
| B.S.E. | Please describe why you are interested in studying engineering at Princeton. Include any of your experiences in or exposure to engineering, and how you think the programs offered at the University suit your particular interests. | 250 词以内 |

> 注：Princeton 工程申请走 **B.S.E.（Bachelor of Science in Engineering）** 路径，对应本页所说的工程专项提示。

#### Your Voice

| 题号 | Prompt | 字数 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Princeton values community and encourages students, faculty, staff and leadership to engage in respectful conversations that can expand their perspectives and challenge their ideas and beliefs. As a prospective member of this community, reflect on how your lived experiences will impact the conversations you will have in the classroom, the dining hall or other campus spaces. What lessons have you learned in life thus far? What will your classmates learn from you? In short, how has your lived experience shaped you? | 500 词以内 |
| 2 | Princeton has a longstanding commitment to understanding our responsibility to society through service and civic engagement. How does your own story intersect with these ideals? | 250 词以内 |

#### More About You（每题 50 词以内）

| 题号 | Prompt |
|---|---|
| 1 | What is a new skill you would like to learn in college? |
| 2 | What brings you joy? |
| 3 | What song represents the soundtrack of your life at this moment? |

### Graded Written Paper 要求
- 必须提交 1 份带老师评分/评语的学术写作样本
- 最好来自英语、历史、社科课程
- 1–2 页即可
- 要看说明文（expository writing），不偏好创意写作

## 标化考试

| 项目 | 政策 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| SAT / ACT（fall 2026, 2027 entry） | **Test-optional** | 不提交不会处于劣势 |
| SAT / ACT（自 2027-28 admission cycle 起） | **恢复必交** | 对应 fall 2028 entry |
| 送分代码 | SAT: 2672；ACT: 2588 | 需由考试机构直送 |
| Score Choice | 允许 | Princeton 接受 SAT score choice |
| ACT 取分 | 最高 composite | 鼓励提交全部成绩 |
| AP / IB | 推荐自报 | 作为补充学术信息 |
| 英语考试 | 特定国际申请人必需 | TOEFL / IELTS / DET / PTE |

## 面试

| 项目 | 说明 |
|---|---|
| 是否提供面试 | 提供 **optional alumni interview** |
| 是否必需 | 否 |
| 可否主动退出 | 可以；在 Princeton-specific Questions 中 opt out |
| 不面试是否吃亏 | **不会**。官网明确选择退出或未获分配均不构成劣势 |
| 安排方式 | Application 被收到后，由 Princeton Alumni Schools Committee 校友联系 |
| 时长 | 一般 30–45 分钟 |
| 地点 | 不提供 on-campus interview |

---

## 提交后流程

1. **确认邮件**：提交 admission application 后约 **24–48 小时**收到 Applicant Portal 访问信息
2. **Portal 追踪**：可在 Princeton Applicant Portal 查看 checklist 是否齐全
3. **补交缺件**：系统会提示缺失材料；从 10 月中旬起可稳定在线追踪
4. **面试**：若未 opt out 且当地有校友资源，可能收到面试邀请
5. **Midyear**：高三上成绩可用后由 counselor 提交 midyear report
6. **等待结果**：SCEA 12 月中旬；RD 3 月下旬；录取后 5 月 1 日前回复
